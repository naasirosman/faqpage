Zoro Account FAQs
  • Don't worry. You can reset your password by:
    1. Clicking 'Log in' on the top right-hand corner.
    2. A pop up window will appear, on the bottom right click 'Forgot Password?'.
    3. Enter the email address you registered with.
    4. You will then receive a confirmation email in response.
    5. Follow the steps advised in the email.
    Please Note: If you do not receive an email, Please check your spam folder.
